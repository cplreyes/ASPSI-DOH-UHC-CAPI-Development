# Source - UHC Y2 Pre-Testing Plan

The UHC Survey **Year 2 Pre-Testing Plan** (REI No. 2025-001). Defines the survey's expanded scope and the pretest design — the validation step that gates Deliverable 2 and is the precondition for full fieldwork. Read via the Drive link in the DOH/ASPSI pretest-coordination email.

## Survey scope (Year 2 vs Year 1)

- **Purpose:** generate evidence on the public's experience of UHC to monitor/evaluate the **UHC Act + IRR**; build on Year 1.
- **Expanded coverage:** **37 newly designated UHC Integration Sites → 107 UHC IS total**, plus **13 non-UHC IS**.
- **New focus:** the effect of the **progressive PhilHealth benefits 2024→2025** on **financial risk protection, healthcare service delivery, and beneficiary experience** *(this is the policy backdrop to the PhilHealth-reinstatement questions — see [[Source - PhilHealth Reinstatement Email (Kidd 2026-06-09)]])*.
- **Representation:** national, regional, and local — provincial, **HUCs**, and **ICCs**.
- **Objectives:** (1) comprehensive data collection; (2) a data-analysis framework enhancing DOH data utilization; (3) dissemination to stakeholders.

## Pretest design

- **Five respondent types** (one per instrument): **facility head (F1) · healthcare worker (F2) · inpatient + outpatient (F3 Patient) · household head / main decision-maker (F4)**. Each questionnaire is pretested against target characteristics (sample sizing referencing Fisher; per-type counts in the plan's Tables 1–2).
- **Pretest sites — Los Baños and Bay, Laguna** (chosen for proximity/accessibility):
  - Facility/patient: the **rural health unit** + **St. Jude Family Hospital (Los Baños)**.
  - **Household survey: Barangays Bayog and Mayondon, Los Baños, Laguna.**
- **Final instruments** go to **PSA** and the **DOH Ethics Review Board / SJREB** for approval; the **final versions are converted to tablet-based (CAPI) formats** for data collection.
- **Data-collection protocol:** advance official communication + proposed visit schedule to each sampled facility → facility visit → patient listing (temporary workstation at the facility) → informed consent → interview (companion may assist where the patient cannot).
- Ethics contact in the consent materials: **SJREB, DOH** — sjreb.doh@gmail.com.

## Relevance to the CAPI build

- Confirms the **5-instrument / respondent-type mapping** the CSPro tools implement (F1/F2/F3/F4) and the patient-listing flow ([[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/sources/Source - Annex F3b Patient Listing Protocol]]).
- The pretest is the **field-readiness validation** Carl's instruments feed into; its scheduling sits in the ASPSI/DOH lane and is gated by SJREB clearance + the updated CAPI ([[Source - Project Movement and Revised Timeline (Apr-Jun 2026)]]).
- Pretest location (**Los Baños/Bay, Laguna**) is the real pretest geography — distinct from the **Laguna (Cabuyao/Biñan) PSGC keys** used for UAT device-testing.
