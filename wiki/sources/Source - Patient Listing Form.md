# Source — Patient Listing Form

Single-page form used to list and recruit patient respondents for the [[wiki/sources/Source - Annex F3 Patient Survey Questionnaire|Patient Survey (F3)]]. Used at each sampled health facility before conducting patient interviews.

## Structure

- **Header fields**: Region, Province, Site (UHC IS / non-UHC IS), Facility Name
- **Respondent type**: Outpatient or Inpatient (checkbox)
- **Listing table**: 25 rows with columns for Family Name (optional) and Contact Number

## Purpose

Enumerators use this form to systematically list eligible patients at a facility, then apply the sampling protocol to select interviewees. The form documents the sampling frame at each facility for audit and quality control purposes.

## CAPI Development Notes

- This is a field logistics form, not a survey instrument — it may remain paper-based rather than being converted to CSPro.
- If digitized, it would be a simple roster with facility identification fields.
- Links to the patient replacement protocol (Annex D of the Inception Report).
