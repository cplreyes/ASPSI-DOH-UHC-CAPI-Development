## Standard CV structure (per expert)

- **Position** + **Name** + **Profession** + **Date of Birth** + **Nationality**
- **Key Qualifications** — narrative summary of expertise
- **Education** — degree list with institution and year
- **Employment Record** — date-ranged positions with institution, references, summary of activities
- **Languages** — table (Speaking/Reading/Writing per language)
- **Project experience** — past engagements relevant to the assignment
