## Context from [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/sources/Source - Revised Inception Report|Revised IR]] Section V.C

Three of the sites (Pateros, Negros Oriental, Siquijor) have **pending integration applications** as of November 2025; the remaining 10 are explicitly enumerated in the IR body text in the same order:

- NCR — Makati City
- Region III — Angeles City, Olongapo City
- CALABARZON (IV-A) — Cavite
- Bicol (V) — Camarines Sur
- Region VIII — Tacloban City
- Region IX — Sulu
- BARMM — Lanao Del Sur, Tawi-Tawi, Cotabato City

## F4 Household Survey allocation

Per [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/sources/Source - Revised Inception Report|Revised IR]] Table 4, the 13 non-UHC IS map to island groups as follows:

- **Northern/Central Luzon:** Makati City, Angeles City, Olongapo City
- **Southern Luzon:** Cavite, Camarines Sur
- **Visayas:** Negros Oriental, Siquijor, Tacloban City
- **Mindanao:** Sulu, Cotabato City, Lanao del Sur, Tawi-Tawi

4 non-UHC IS are randomly selected per island group (see [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/sources/Source - Revised Inception Report|IR]] Section V.D).
