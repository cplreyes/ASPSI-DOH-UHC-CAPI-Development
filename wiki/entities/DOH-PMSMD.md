# DOH-PMSMD — Performance Monitoring and Strategy Management Division

Division within the Department of Health (DOH) responsible for monitoring and evaluating the implementation of the UHC Act (RA 11223). The client and counterpart agency for the [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/concepts/UHC Survey Year 2|UHC Survey Year 2]].

## Key Personnel

- **Mr. Lindsley Jeremiah D. Villarante** — Division Chief. Primary DOH counterpart for the survey.

## Role in the Project

- Commissioned [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/entities/ASPSI|ASPSI]] under REI NO. 2025-001.
- Provides technical review of indicators and questionnaires.
- Coordinates survey clearances, ethics review, and stakeholder engagement.
- Receives all deliverables and survey data.

## Related Entities

- DOH Epidemiology Bureau (Dr. Alfonso Miguel Regala) — technical input on indicators.
- [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/entities/IDinsight|IDinsight]] (Mr. Alec Lim) — conducted Year 1 survey, shared findings; see [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/sources/Source - IDinsight UHC Survey 2024 Final Report]].
- ADB (Ms. Xylee Javier) — technical recommendations for Year 2.
- WHO (Dr. Duan Mengjuan) — advisory role.

## Relationship to Carl's Compensation

Although Carl is not directly engaged by DOH, his payments are **gated by DOH acceptance** of each deliverable bundle. CSA §5 explicitly allows ASPSI to withhold payment to Carl if DOH refuses payment to ASPSI. This makes DOH-PMSMD the de facto acceptor of Carl's work even though there is no direct contractual relationship. See [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/sources/Source - Signed CSA Dec 15 2025]].
