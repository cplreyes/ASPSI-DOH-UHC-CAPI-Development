# US Census Bureau

The United States Census Bureau (part of the U.S. Department of Commerce) develops and maintains [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/concepts/CSPro|CSPro]] (Census and Survey Processing System), a free, open-source software package for entering, editing, tabulating, and disseminating census and survey data. CSPro has been distributed by the Bureau since the 1980s and is widely used by national statistical offices, ministries, and survey organizations worldwide.

## Role in this project

- **Software author** — CSPro 8.0, CSEntry, and CSWeb are all Census Bureau products. The project's CAPI build for [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/concepts/UHC Survey Year 2|UHC Survey Year 2]] runs entirely on this stack.
- **Documentation publisher** — Maintains the canonical user guides, logic reference, and CAPI/sync walkthroughs at https://www.census.gov/data/software/cspro/documentation.html (see [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/sources/Source - CSPro Documentation|Source - CSPro Documentation]]).
- **Support channel** — Offers free email support at `cspro@lists.census.gov` (English, French, or Spanish).
