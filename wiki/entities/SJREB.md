# SJREB — Single Joint Research Ethics Board

The ethics review board responsible for issuing ethical clearance for the [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/concepts/UHC Survey Year 2|UHC Survey Year 2]]. SJREB clearance is a hard prerequisite for pre-testing and field data collection — no human-subject contact may occur until clearance is granted.

## Role in the Project

- Reviews the survey protocol, instruments, and informed consent materials.
- Issues the ethical clearance that gates pretesting → CAPI field validation → training → field deployment.
- Requires **CHD (Center for Health Development) coordination** as part of its process (per ASPSI Mar 6 communication).
- Will not act on a submission until **DOH provides technical clearance**, which DOH itself withholds until **Tranche 1 (Inception Report) is formally accepted**.

## Why SJREB matters to Carl

SJREB sits at the center of the project's critical path blocker:

```
Tranche 1 acceptance → DOH technical clearance → SJREB ethics clearance → Pretesting → CAPI bench/field validation → Tranche 2 deliverable acceptance → Carl's C-T2 payment (PHP 117,000)
```

Because pre-tested tools are part of [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/sources/Source - Signed CSA Dec 15 2025|Carl's C-T2]] deliverable bundle, SJREB clearance is not just a downstream blocker — it directly gates 30% of Carl's contract value.
