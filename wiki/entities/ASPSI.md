# ASPSI — Asian Social Project Services, Inc.

Consulting firm contracted by [[wiki/entities/DOH-PMSMD|DOH-PMSMD]] to conduct the [[wiki/concepts/UHC Survey Year 2|UHC Survey Year 2]] under REI NO. 2025-001 (November 2025 – August 2026).

## Role in the Project

ASPSI is the implementing organization responsible for the full survey lifecycle: inception report, questionnaire development, CAPI system development, enumerator training, field data collection, data processing, and report preparation.
