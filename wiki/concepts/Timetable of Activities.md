# Timetable of Activities

The official UHC Survey Year 2 schedule, as defined in **Table 14** of the Revised Inception Report (Source: [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/sources/Source - Revised Inception Report]]). Covers the 9-month engagement from **Notice to Proceed (Nov 14, 2025)** to **project close (Aug 14, 2026)**.

> [!note] Source format
> Table 14 is embedded in the Inception Report as two PNG images (rId23 and rId26), not as a Word table. Week-by-week shading is only viewable in the source PDF/DOCX. This page captures the structured content; refer to the original for the visual Gantt.

## Deliverables and Submission Dates

| # | Deliverable | Target Date | Month |
|---|---|---|---|
| D1 | Approved Inception Report (work plan, budget, methodology, sampling, survey design, data collection protocols, data processing/analysis plans, field operations) | ~Dec 5, 2025 | Month 1 |
| D2 | Approved revised operating procedures (final survey QXs, instruction manuals, data processing/analysis plan, e-data processing/analysis plan, ethics clearance documentation) | ~Dec 31, 2025 | Month 2 |
| D3 | Approved progress report (data presentation) summarizing the results of piloting and an initial preliminary report | ~Jul 10, 2026 | Month 8 |
| D4 | Approved documentation of training (training materials + summary report w/ pre & post assessment), Approved final report, summary data/results, codes for the UHC survey, and documentation of dissemination meetings | ~Aug 15, 2026 | Month 9 |

See also [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/sources/Source - Signed CSA Dec 15 2025]] for the deliverable-linked payment tranches and the 1%/day late penalty exposure tied to these dates.

## A. Primary Activities

### 1. Team Mobilization and Preparation of Inception (Month 1)
1.1 Inception meeting
1.2 Preparation of the Draft Inception Report
1.3 Inception Kick-off meeting with [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/entities/DOH-PMSMD|DOH]]
1.4 Review of documents shared by DOH (incl. DOH technical review of indicators and data collection tools)
1.5 Submission of the Inception Report
1.6 DOH-PMSMD reviews submitted Inception Report and provides feedback
1.7 Revision and finalization of the Inception Report
1.8 Submission of the Final Inception Report and approval by DOH-PMSMD

### 2. Development of Survey Materials and Submission of Survey Protocol to PSA-SSRECS and Research Ethics Board (Months 1–2)
2.1 Selection of area, coordination and preparations for the conduct of pre-test
2.2 Pre-testing of survey questionnaires
2.3 Refinement of survey questionnaires based on pre-test results
2.4 Development of survey manuals/procedures (incl. definition of terms and other materials needed for recruiting survey interviewers)
2.5 Submission and approval of the survey protocol (incl. survey questionnaire) to [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/entities/PSA|PSA]]-SSRECS and [[1_Projects/ASPSI-DOH-CAPI-CSPro-Development/wiki/entities/SJREB|SJREB]] for the conduct of the survey

### 3. Conversion of paper survey questionnaires into CAPI form (Months 1–3)

### 4. Bench-testing of the CAPI application (Months 2–3)

### 5. Recruitment, mobilization, and training of survey teams (Months 3–4)

### 6. Refinement and finalization of the CAPI instrument (incl. translations as needed) (Months 3–4)

### 7. Coordination with LGUs, social and logistical arrangements for the conduct of the survey (continuing)

## B. Conduct of Survey (Months 4–7)

- Deployment of field supervisors and survey enumerators
- Actual conduct of facility-head, healthcare worker, patient, and community surveys
- Continuous monitoring, supervision, and regular supervisory activities
- Preparation and submission of progress report (initial/preliminary report on data collection)

## C. Post-Survey Activities (Months 7–9)

- Cleaning of data from data collection operations
- Data validation
- Data processing and analysis
- Preparation of the survey report
- Presentation of the survey results to DOH-PMSMD
- Preparatory activities and actual conduct of "Stakeholder's Forum"
- Preparation of survey final report (incl. summary data/analysis tools for the UHC survey and documentation of dissemination meetings)
