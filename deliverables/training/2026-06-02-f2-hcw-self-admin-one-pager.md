# F2 Healthcare Worker Survey — How to Complete It (One-Pager)

> **Format note.** Designed to print on a **single page** (or send as an image/PDF) and share with healthcare workers alongside their survey link. Respondent-facing, plain language. Placeholders in `[ ]` are filled per facility before distribution. Render to a clean one-pager via `pandoc` (add the facility logo/header in the master template).

---

## 📋 You've been invited to the UHC Healthcare Worker Survey

The Department of Health (DOH), through ASPSI, is conducting the **Universal Health Care (UHC) Survey**. Your views as a healthcare worker matter. The survey is **short, voluntary, and confidential**, and you complete it **yourself** on your own phone, tablet, or computer — no interviewer needed.

---

## ▶️ How to start

1. **Open your survey link.** Tap the link shared in your facility's [Viber / Messenger / email] group:
   **[your facility's survey link here]**
2. It opens in your web browser — **nothing to download or install.**
3. *(Optional)* For easier access, tap your browser's menu → **"Add to Home Screen"** to save it like an app. You can also just use the link.
4. **Read the consent screen.** It explains what the survey is for and what's collected. The questionnaire unlocks **only after** you agree to continue.

---

## ✍️ While you answer

- Answer at **your own pace** — there's no timer per question.
- The form **only shows questions that apply to you** and will gently flag an answer that looks off, so it's easy to get right.
- **Your answers save automatically.** You can stop and come back later — **on the same device and browser** — and pick up where you left off.
- A 📶 weak connection is fine: the form saves your answers and submits when you're back online.

---

## ✅ Finishing & your completion window

- Answer through to the end and tap **Submit**.
- You'll see a clear **"Submitted" confirmation** once it's safely received — that means you're done.
- Please complete it **within [3] days** of first opening the link.

> **No "Submitted" confirmation?** You're not done yet — re-open the link on the **same device** to resume and submit.

---

## 🔒 Your privacy

- Your responses are **confidential** and used only for the UHC Survey.
- ASPSI is registered with the **National Privacy Commission (PIC-000-358-2021)** and handles your data under the Data Privacy Act (RA 10173).
- The consent screen tells you exactly what is collected (including a submission timestamp/location) before you agree.
- Taking part is **voluntary** — it does not affect your employment or your work in any way.

---

## ❓ Need help?

| If… | Then… |
|---|---|
| The link won't open | Try another browser, or ask your facility's survey contact for a fresh link |
| You closed it midway | Re-open the **same link on the same device** — your answers are saved |
| You're not sure it submitted | Look for the **"Submitted"** confirmation; if it's missing, re-open and submit |
| Any other question | Contact your facility survey focal person: **[name / number]** |

---

*Paper option:* If you can't complete it online, ask your facility focal person for a **paper copy** — hand it back before the survey team leaves, and ASPSI will encode it for you.

**Thank you for helping improve Universal Health Care in the Philippines. 🇵🇭**
