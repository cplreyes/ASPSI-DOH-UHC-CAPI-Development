### Form 3 — `FC_CAPTURE` (GPS + Verification Photo)

**Source record:** triggers here; values persisted to `REC_FACILITY_CAPTURE` (off-form, type Z).
**Purpose:** geo-tag the facility and capture a verification photo before data entry.

| Row | Field | Control | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Label "Capture facility GPS" | Section label | — |
| 2 | `GPS_TRIGGER` | Button (Yes/No flag) | `onfocus` calls `ReadGPSReading()` from `shared/Capture-Helpers.apc` |
| 3 | `GPS_STATUS` (display) | Read-only text | Shows lat/long/accuracy from `REC_FACILITY_CAPTURE` once set |
| 4 | Label "Take verification photo" | Section label | — |
| 5 | `PHOTO_TRIGGER` | Button (Yes/No flag) | `onfocus` calls `TakeVerificationPhoto()`; filename = `case-{QUESTIONNAIRE_NO}-verification.jpg` |
| 6 | `PHOTO_STATUS` (display) | Read-only text | Shows captured filename |

**Row cost:** 7 · **Budget fit:** ✅ · **Helpers:** `shared/Capture-Helpers.apc`

> **Placement rationale:** capture runs immediately after PSGC because the interviewer is positioned to geo-tag and photograph the facility entrance before sitting down with the facility head. If the user is blocked from capture (e.g., no photo permission), they can skip and return.

### Forms 7–11 — Section C (UHC Implementation, Q9–Q50)

Section C is too large for one form (~42 questions, most with `_OTHER_TXT` companions). Split at skip-logic boundaries into five forms:

| Form | Label | Q range | Topic |
|---|---|---|---|
| 7 | `C1_PRIMARY_PKG` | Q9–Q12 | Primary care package awareness / availability |
| 8 | `C2_UNITS_ROLES` | Q13–Q22 | PHU / HPU / new roles / new departments (all skip-gated from prior Y/N) |
| 9 | `C3_FACILITY_CHANGES` | Q23–Q30 | Buildings / rooms / equipment / supplies |
| 10 | `C4_EMR_DATA` | Q31–Q38 | EMR, data submission, staffing, referral changes |
| 11 | `C5_AWARENESS_CHALL` | Q39–Q50 | UHC awareness, NBB/ZBB, challenges (Q49/Q50 select-all) |

**Design notes:**
- Every Q*_OTHER_TXT lives on the same form as its parent.
- Select-all (Q49, Q50) renders as a checkbox list; each checkbox emits a `Q*_OPT_{n}` flag in `postproc`. Mutually-exclusive "IDK" / "Other" rules enforced in `postproc`.
- Gates (e.g., Q11 enabled iff Q10 = Yes) implemented via `onfocus` on the dependent field, not tab-order tricks.

### Forms 12–15 — Section D (YAKAP / Konsulta, Q51–Q100)

Section D has two disjoint paths via Q51. Split into four forms with the Q51 gate as the branch point:

| Form | Label | Q range | Path |
|---|---|---|---|
| 12 | `D1_ACCRED_GATE` | Q51 + Q52 | Accreditation status + date; Q51 gates the whole section |
| 13 | `D2_ACCRED_OPS` | Q52–Q78 | Accredited path: capitation, payment, challenges |
| 14 | `D3_NONACCRED` | Q79–Q84 | Non-accredited path: intent to accredit |
| 15 | `D4_COSTING_PAYMENTS` | Q85–Q100 | Costing, payments, eligibility counts, Q99/Q100 (Q51=Yes only) |

**Design notes:**
- Q51 gate drives form-level skip: if Q51 = No, skip Form 13 entirely, go to Form 14.
- Q65 option selections gate Q66–Q74 at the field level (CSPro `onfocus` per dependent field), since all nine are display-logic toggles on one form (Form 13 subsection).
- `Q87 / Q86` ratio check (registered ≤ eligible) runs in `postproc` of Form 15.

**Open:** Form 13 likely needs to split further (Q52–Q78 is 27 items). Candidate internal split: Q52–Q64 (capitation) + Q65–Q78 (challenges). Decide when building.

## 3. Form order (tab order)

```
1  FC_METADATA           ← case start, consent gate
2  FC_GEO                ← PSGC cascade
3  FC_CAPTURE            ← GPS + photo
4  A1_PROFILE            ← Section A (head profile)
5  A2_CONSENT_CONTACTS
6  B_FACILITY            ← Section B
7  C1_PRIMARY_PKG        ← Section C split 1/5
8  C2_UNITS_ROLES
9  C3_FACILITY_CHANGES
10 C4_EMR_DATA
11 C5_AWARENESS_CHALL
12 D1_ACCRED_GATE        ← Section D gate
13 D2_ACCRED_OPS         ← Q51 = Yes branch
14 D3_NONACCRED          ← Q51 = No branch
15 D4_COSTING_PAYMENTS
16 E1_BUCAS              ← Section E
17 E2_GAMOT
18 F1_LICENSING_GATE     ← Section F
19 F2_Q121_GRID
20 G1_NBB                ← Section G
21 G2_ZBB
22 G3_LGU
23 G4_REFERRAL
24 H_HR                  ← Section H
25 SEC_HOSP_CENSUS       ← secondary data (stubs)
26 SEC_HCW_ROSTER
27 SEC_YK_SERVICES
28 SEC_LAB_PRICES
29 CLOSING               ← disposition + submit
```

**Skip behavior at the form level:**
- Consent = No (Form 1) → jump to Form 29.
- Tenure < 6mo (Form 4) → set disposition = Refused/Incomplete, jump to Form 29.
- Q51 = No (Form 12) → skip Form 13, proceed to Form 14.
- Q51 = Yes (Form 12) → skip Form 14, proceed to Form 15 after Form 13.

All other skips are intra-form GATE logic (field-level enabled/disabled), not form-level jumps.
