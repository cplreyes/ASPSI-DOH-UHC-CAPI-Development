Step-by-step guides

Full walkthroughs for the people who run and take the surveys:

  • CSEntry on your tablet — enumerator guide For field enumerators (F1 · F3 · F4): install, connect, download the survey, run an interview, and sync.
  • Healthcare Worker survey — how to complete it For healthcare workers (F2, self-administered): opening the link, answering, finishing, and privacy.
  • F1 Facility Head — paper ↔ CAPI comparison (complete) All 10 sections (Q1–Q166): every question beside its real CSEntry screen, with notes on skip logic, validations, and where the tablet differs. Complete — only the check-box screen is still being captured.
  • F3 Patient — paper ↔ CAPI comparison (complete) All 13 sections (A–L): every question beside the matching CSEntry control, with appearance and validation notes. Complete — the check-box screen is still being captured.
  • F4 Household — paper ↔ CAPI comparison (complete) All 17 sections (A–Q), including the household roster and expenditure grids: every question beside the matching CSEntry control. Complete — the check-box and grid screens are still being captured.
  • F2 Healthcare Worker — paper ↔ web (PWA) comparison (complete) All sections of the printed HCW questionnaire beside the real self-administered web (PWA) screens — fully captured from the live web app.
  • Sync Dashboard — synced cases at a glance For supervisors/monitors: charts of uploaded F1/F3/F4 cases — totals plus breakdowns by region, facility, ownership, patient type, sex, and result of visit. Refreshes automatically as cases sync.

The two systems

The platform has two separate systems, one per group of instruments. Each has its own sign-in.

  • CSWeb — the server for the Facility Head (F1), Patient (F3), and Household (F4) surveys. Enumerators collect these on Android tablets in CSEntry; supervisors and monitors watch the data arrive here.
  • HCW Admin Portal — administration for the self-administered Healthcare Worker (F2) survey, which respondents fill in on the web.

Signing in

From the home page, choose your system. Each system uses separate credentials — a CSWeb account does not work on the HCW portal, and vice versa.

  • CSWeb: open csweb.asiansocial.org and sign in with the username and password issued to you.
  • Enumerators use the same CSWeb account inside the CSEntry app on the tablet (see “Collecting on a tablet”).
Don’t have an account? Accounts are issued by your survey coordinator. Contact them rather than sharing a login — each person needs their own.

Using CSWeb (monitoring)

After signing in to CSWeb, the left menu has the tabs you’ll use to follow fieldwork:

  • Data Browse the cases that have synced from the tablets, per instrument. Open a case to see its answers.
  • Sync Report A running count of uploaded cases by facility. Pick an instrument from the sub-menu; the table lists each questionnaire number with its facility name and total, and “View case” opens the record.
  • Map Report Plots cases that captured a GPS fix on a map of the Philippines — useful for confirming coverage by area.
  • Apps The CAPI applications that enumerators download to their tablets. New questionnaire versions are published here.

Users, Roles, and Settings are for administrators — managing accounts and access. Most field and monitoring staff won’t need them.

Collecting on a tablet (enumerators)

  1. Install CSEntry from Google Play (publisher U.S. Census Bureau, free). Allow Location and Camera when asked — both are needed for GPS and verification photos.
  2. In CSEntry, add your survey from the server. Server address: https://csweb.asiansocial.org/csweb/api. Sign in with your own CSWeb account and download your instrument (FacilityHeadSurvey, PatientSurvey, or HouseholdSurvey).
  3. Run interviews. Cases save on the device, so you can work without a connection and sync later.
  4. Keep the app current: ⋮ menu → Update Installed Applications when you have internet, so you always run the latest questionnaire.

Syncing data

Syncing sends your new and changed cases to the server. Do it whenever you have a stable connection — at least once at the end of each field day.

  1. From your survey’s case list, choose Synchronize (the circular-arrows icon).
  2. Sign in with your own account if prompted.
  3. Wait for “Sync complete / success.” Only new or changed cases are sent, so it’s quick.

Your supervisor or monitor can then confirm the case appears in CSWeb → Sync Report, and — if the GPS got a real outdoor fix — that a pin shows on the Map. An indoor case may have a blank GPS; that’s expected.

Troubleshooting

  • Can’t connect or download: check your internet, and type the server address exactly, including https:// and /csweb/api.
  • Login fails: re-type the username in lowercase with no spaces, and confirm the password.
  • Screens look old (one question per screen): you’re on an older version — ⋮ → Update Installed Applications, or remove and re-add the app.
  • GPS won’t read: move near a window or step outside, and make sure Location is allowed and switched on. Indoors it may time out and stay blank — note it and continue.
  • Camera won’t open: allow Camera in Settings → Apps → CSEntry → Permissions.
  • Sync fails: confirm internet, re-enter your account, and retry. If it still fails, capture a screenshot of the error for your coordinator.

Support

For account credentials, field issues, or anything not covered here, contact your survey coordinator or team supervisor. When reporting a problem, a screenshot or photo of the screen and the questionnaire number involved helps us resolve it faster.
