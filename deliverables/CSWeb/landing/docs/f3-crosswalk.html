A. Introduction and Informed Consent

Section A — Q1–Q3, paper page 3 — identifies who is answering: the patient, or a companion answering on their behalf.

Q1–Q3 Who is answering?

On paper

1. Are you the patient?  ·  2. What is your relationship to the patient?  ·  3. Do you live in the same house as the patient?

  • Q1: Yes (→ skip to Q4) · No
  • Q2: Spouse · Son · Daughter · Father · Mother · … 20 options incl. “Other” / “Refuse”
  • Q3: Yes · No

On the tablet (CAPI)

Single-selects (shown) for Q1/Q2 and a Yes / No for Q3. If Q1 = Yes (the respondent is the patient), Q2–Q3 are skipped and the interview jumps to Q4.

B. Patient Profile

On the tablet (CAPI)

Q53 is the gate: Q53 = No (no PCP) skips Q54–Q62 and jumps to Q63. Q54 is a short single-select; its Other-specify text box opens only when Q54 = Other. Q55–Q57 are Yes/No.

Q58–Q62 Appointment wait & modes of communication

On paper

58. Wait time to set an appointment (Days / Minutes)  ·  59. Mode/s of communication available when scheduling  ·  60. If teleconsult was available, did you succeed in using it (scheduling)?  ·  61. Mode/s of communication available when consulting  ·  62. If teleconsult was available, did you succeed in using it (consultation)?

On the tablet (CAPI)
