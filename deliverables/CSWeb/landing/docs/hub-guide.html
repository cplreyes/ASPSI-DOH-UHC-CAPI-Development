The Supervisor & Enumerator Hub — sign in, then pick your task

The hub is one sign-in that opens a menu built for your role, then launches the same Facility Head (F1), Patient (F3), and Household (F4) surveys you already use — and returns you to the menu when you finish. It sits on top of the existing apps; nothing about the surveys changes.

Being rolled out. The direct way of adding F1/F3/F4 in CSEntry (see the Enumerator guide) still works exactly as before — the hub is an optional sign-in layer. A few menu items are marked “coming soon” while the supervisor data-exchange features are finished.

What it is, in one picture

Three steps, then back to the menu for the next task:

  1. Sign in once in the LoginApp with your username and password.
  2. A menu for your role opens (Supervisor or Enumerator).
  3. Pick a survey — it opens the real F1/F3/F4 app, and when you finish you land back on the menu.
Your survey data is unchanged and stays on the device exactly as before. The hub only opens the surveys for you — it never moves or edits your cases. Syncing still works the same way (see below).

1. Installing the hub (one time)

  1. Open CSEntry on your tablet (install it first if needed — see the Enumerator guide, Step 1).
  2. Tap ⋮ → Add Application → From a server, point it at https://csweb.asiansocial.org/csweb, and sign in with your own account.
  3. Add LoginApp (this also installs the menu). Keep your survey apps — FacilityHeadSurvey, PatientSurvey, HouseholdSurvey — installed too; the menu opens them.
To update later: remove LoginApp, then Add Application → From a server again. The “⋮ → Update Installed Applications” option can miss new versions — remove + re-add is the reliable way.

2. Signing in

  1. Open LoginApp.
  2. Type your username and password (issued by your coordinator — keep them private; don’t share a login).
  3. Advance. Your role menu opens, showing your role at the top.

A wrong password or an unknown username tells you and lets you try again. The hub sign-in is a convenience gate — your real, secure login is still your CSWeb account when you sync.

3. The Supervisor menu

After signing in as a supervisor, you’ll see:

  • Assign Enumeration Area — send an EA assignment to an enumerator. (coming soon)
  • Listing Data — view report — review listing coverage. (report opens now; live collected counts coming soon)
  • Survey Interview — view report — review interview coverage. (report opens now; live collected counts coming soon)
  • View EA on Map — see the EA and cases on a map. (coming soon)
  • Open F1 / F3 / F4 (review) — open a survey to spot-check an interview. (available now)
  • Log out — return to the sign-in screen. (available now)

4. The Enumerator menu

After signing in as an enumerator, you’ll see:

  • Listing Exercise — receive your assigned EA, list, and send it to your supervisor. (coming soon)
  • Receive Assigned Data (Patient) — pull the patient list assigned to you. (coming soon)
  • Conduct F1 / F3 / F4 Interview — open the survey and run interviews as usual. (available now)
  • View EA on Map — see your assigned area on a map. (coming soon)
  • View my report — your own coverage so far. (report opens now; live counts coming soon)
  • Log out — return to the sign-in screen. (available now)
If a survey item “does nothing,” that survey app isn’t installed on this tablet yet — Add Application → install FacilityHeadSurvey / PatientSurvey / HouseholdSurvey, then try again from the menu.

5. Syncing data

Syncing is unchanged from the direct workflow: from each survey’s case list, tap Synchronize and sign in with your CSWeb account; only new or changed cases are sent. Do it at least once at the end of each field day. (The supervisor “collect from enumerators / relay to the server” path is part of the coming soon features above.)

Troubleshooting

  • “Incorrect password.” — re-type carefully; if it persists, ask your coordinator to check your account.
  • “Unknown username.” — you may not be in this build yet, or it’s out of date — remove + re-add LoginApp, then ask your coordinator if still missing.
  • “No role found from login…” — close and sign in again through LoginApp (don’t open the menu app on its own).
  • A survey item does nothing — that survey isn’t installed; add it from the server, then retry.
  • Old screens after an update — remove + re-add the app and test on a fresh case.

Support

For credentials, access, or anything not covered here, contact your survey coordinator or team supervisor. A screenshot of the screen and the questionnaire number help us resolve issues faster.
