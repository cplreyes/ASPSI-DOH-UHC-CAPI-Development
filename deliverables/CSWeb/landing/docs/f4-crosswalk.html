C. Household Characteristics (roster)

Section C — Q30–Q50, paper pages 6–7 — a repeating household roster: the interviewer captures one full set of questions (name, presence, age, sex, relationship, disability, civil status, education, employment, and every insurance coverage) for each household member. On paper this is a wide grid filled row-by-row; on the tablet CSEntry walks member-by-member, looping the same screen-set up to 20 times (one occurrence per member). A single household-level gate (Q47) decides whether the private-insurance sub-roster (Q48–Q50) is asked at all.

On the tablet (CAPI)

This roster is the loop driver — CSEntry repeats this whole screen-set once per member (record C_HOUSEHOLD_ROSTER, max 20 occurrences), capturing one member fully before advancing to the next.

CAPI differsOn paper this is a grid the interviewer fills row-by-row across the page; on the tablet CSEntry walks member-by-member, presenting one member's full column of questions at a time before looping to the next member.
